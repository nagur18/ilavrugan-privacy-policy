üîí Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how IlavRugan Auto Poster collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service.

1. Introduction

Welcome to IlavRugan Auto Poster ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information when you use our automation service for posting Amazon affiliate products to Pinterest.

By using IlavRugan Auto Poster, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 OAuth Authentication Data

When you connect your Pinterest account to our service, we collect:

  • OAuth access tokens provided by Pinterest
  • Basic Pinterest account information (username, user ID)
  • Board information for posting content

2.2 Product and Posting Data

We process the following information to provide our service:

  • Amazon product URLs and identifiers you select for posting
  • Generated captions and descriptions
  • Product images (processed and posted to Pinterest)
  • Posting logs and timestamps

2.3 Technical Information

We may collect basic technical information for service functionality:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system information
  • IP address (for security purposes only)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To generate and post content to your Pinterest account as requested
  • Content Generation: To create AI-powered captions and optimize product images
  • Logging and Analytics: To track posting activity and provide you with usage logs
  • Service Improvement: To maintain and improve the quality of our automation service
  • Security: To protect against unauthorized access and ensure service security

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

We only interact with the following third-party services as necessary to provide our service:

  • Pinterest: OAuth authentication and content posting through Pinterest's official API
  • Amazon: Retrieving publicly available product information through Amazon Product Advertising API
  • AI Services: Processing product data to generate captions (data is not stored by these services)

We may disclose your information only if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously and implement the following measures:

  • OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely
  • All data transmission uses secure HTTPS connections
  • Access to user data is restricted to essential service operations only
  • Regular security audits and updates to protect against vulnerabilities

Data Retention: We retain your OAuth tokens and posting logs only as long as you use our service. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data and revoke access to your Pinterest account
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Revocation: Revoke Pinterest OAuth access at any time through your Pinterest account settings

7. Cookies and Tracking

IlavRugan Auto Poster uses minimal cookies only for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Maintaining your preferences

We do NOT use tracking cookies for advertising or analytics purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending an email notification for material changes (if you've provided your email)
