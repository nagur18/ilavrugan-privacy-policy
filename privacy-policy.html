IlavRugan Auto Poster — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 28, 2025
Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how you can control it.

1. Data Controller

IlavRugan Auto Poster (developer: Nagur). Contact: nagurx18@gmail.com. We are responsible for the processing of your data described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Pinterest (OAuth)

When you connect Pinterest, we collect the OAuth access token and basic Pinterest account information required to post content on your behalf (username, user ID, board information). We do not collect Pinterest passwords.

2.2 Product and Posting Data

Data related to the posts you request:

  • Amazon product URLs/identifiers you select
  • Generated captions and images created for Pins
  • Posting logs, timestamps, and status

2.3 Technical and Support Data

We may collect: browser type, IP address (for security), and minimal diagnostic logs to troubleshoot issues.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and post Pins on your behalf (service provision)
  • To generate captions and optimize images (AI processing)
  • To maintain logs and provide support
  • To prevent abuse and secure the service

4. Third Parties and Sharing

We interact with the following third parties only as necessary:

  • Pinterest: OAuth and posting via Pinterest API.
  • Amazon: Product information via Amazon Product Advertising API.
  • AI providers: To generate captions/images. We limit what is shared and do not store inputs unnecessarily — see retention below.

We do not sell personal data. We may disclose data when required by law or to protect legal rights.

5. Storage, Security & Retention

OAuth tokens and posting logs are stored only as long as you use the service. Tokens are protected using industry-standard measures (encrypted storage and secure transport). All network connections use HTTPS/TLS.

If you stop using the service or request deletion, we will remove your tokens and posting logs within a reasonable timeframe (typically within 30 days) unless retention is required by law.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data and revoke our access.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, contact nagurx18@gmail.com. We will respond within 48 hours.

7. How to Revoke Access

You can revoke OAuth access at any time from your Pinterest account settings. To request deletion of any remaining data we hold, email nagurx18@gmail.com.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for session management and authentication. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for users 18+. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe we have collected such data, contact us to remove it.

10. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in locations outside your country. We take steps to ensure adequate safeguards to protect your data in accordance with this policy.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy; material changes will be posted here with an updated date.
